About

I build ranking queues on messy search data for SEO and content teams. I care more about a useful top-20 than a pretty ROC curve.

Right now that work lives in my FlyRank ML internship: which pages to refresh when the inventory is too big to eyeball. I keep a transparent rule baseline, compare a learned ranker on precision@K, hunt leakage, and use claim language that stays inside the evidence.

What you can check on this site without asking me: a 30k-page refresh queue with a baseline lift (P@50 0.74 vs 0.24 on the starter run), reason codes, and stated limits — plus the public repo and a one-click Colab notebook.
