## Steps to add one case (reuse Week 2 three-beat shape)

1. **Open the Claude Project** — do not start a cold chat. Paste: “New case. Same voice. Three beats only.”
2. **Write the three beats** (you decide; Claude drafts, you cut):
   - **The problem** — who is stuck, what breaks if they guess wrong.
   - **What I did (and decided)** — unit of analysis, metric, what you refuse to claim.
   - **What came of it** — one observed result + one honest limit + optional “next time.”
3. **Copy** `docs/work/refresh-queue.html` → `docs/work/<slug>.html`. Swap title, meta, three beat sections, figures, repo/Colab links.
4. **Add the home card** on `docs/index.html` (meta line, one-sentence proof, link, optional thumb). Lead with the strongest case if order matters.
5. **Run the stranger test:** can a hiring manager get problem → proof → Email me in under a minute?
6. **Commit + push.** Paste the live case URL into the Claude Project so the next update is cheaper.
