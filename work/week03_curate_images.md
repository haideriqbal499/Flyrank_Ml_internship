## Where I chose real capture over AI

1. **Every chart on Home / Work / Paper** — AI would invent bars; these SVGs come from the internship notebook/pipeline numbers a hiring manager can re-open in Colab.
2. **Home hero** — chose **no image** over a generated texture so the claim and Email me stay first.
3. **Phone proof in the fix-log** — real screenshots of the live layout, not a mockup generator.
4. **About** — no AI headshot. The page is text. If I ever show my face, it will be a real photo I took or approved — not a generated stand-in.

## Rejected (discernment)

**Rejected file:** `work/week03_image_curation/rejected-ai-hero-glass.png`

**What it was:** a generated “neural / glass / purple glow” hero background (classic AI-slop connective tissue).

**Why it died:** It fights the identity kit (cream + one green), steals attention from the Precision@50 charts, and proves nothing about ranked refresh queues. A hiring manager would remember the glow, not the work. Whitespace + real SVGs win.

**Also rejected without keeping files:** AI headshot for About; decorative icon row; abstract “data constellation” for Contact. Same reason — frame, don’t compete.
